İdil Onen

İdil Onen is currently a data member of Portrait of Unbelonging under the supervision of Dr. Zeynep Devrim Gursel. Portrait of Unbelonging is a public facing digital humanities project which examines the migration pattern of the Armenian diaspora by constructing visual genealogies with oral stories, photography, and film.
